LET'S TAKE INCREDIBLE EATS GLOBAL

Incredible Eats

IncrEDIBLE Eats was founded to create the world's first mass-manufacturable and entirely edible cutlery, offering an incredible solution to the plastic problem around the world.

Going Global, Korea First

Incredible Eats chose South Korea for global expansion due to its alignment with local environmental concerns, legislation against single-use plastics, consumer interest, and a growing demand for eco-friendly alternatives.

Greatest Challenge, Language

Incredible Eats has a language barrier ahead of it in Korea. To mitigate it, we'll employ a mix of Koreans, Americans and bilinguils in the team, and the USA team also learn Korean phrases.

Exports, and then Wholly Owned Subsidiary

Incredible Eats will first export its cutlery to South Korea as USA and Korea has a free-trade agreement. Our partner, Dippin' Dots' distribution network will be used at first there. The company will keep customizing the cutlery according to Korean market, and later on will switch to a Wholly Owned Subsidiary model in South Korea.

Transnational Strategy for the Team

The company is going to employ a mix of USA, South Koreans and even people from other countries. We want to train the team about different practices - business and cultural, so that everyone can respect each other and the company can grow.

Customization of Functional Elements

We expect a higher profit in the Korean market than the USA itself as there is less competition and people are conscious about the environment. The company will customize and keep updating its technology, HR policies, flavors and designs, and prices as we learn about what is suitable and seen with excitement in the Korean market.
